About Us

we started As- Sa'ada because it was difficult to find comfortable, quality hijabs and abayas that made us feel confident. We knew it was time to create the kind of hijabs and abayas that we wanted to wear ourselves. As-sa'ada celebrates inclusivity, honesty and simplicity. We offer hijabs and abayas that are made from best-in-class materials and uncompromising craftsmanship which speaks directly to the heart of woman who believe in ‘Beauty in modesty’. Above all , we create with the dream of enlivening our ultimate mission: to inspire you to live a modest life.
